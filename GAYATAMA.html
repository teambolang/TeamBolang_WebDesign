Banyuwangi, East Java

KAWAH IJEN

Kawah Ijen is a stunning natural wonder in East Java! Witness the mesmerising turquoise-coloured sulphur lake and the rare phenomenon of ‘blue fire’ that glows at night. Enjoy a thrilling hike to the crater rim, where you'll be mesmerised by the natural beauty and the lives of the hardy sulphur miners. Don't miss the chance to create unforgettable memories in one of Indonesia's most beautiful destinations. Kawah Ijen awaits you!

Probolinggo, East Java

AIR TERJUN MADAKARIPURA

Madakalipura Waterfall, hidden in the tropical forest of East Java, is a 100-metre-high natural wonder that captivates every visitor. With crystal clear water cascading down and surrounded by steep cliffs, its magical atmosphere creates a soothing harmony of natural sounds. The soft, enveloping mist adds to the enchantment, making this waterfall a photo haven for nature lovers. A spiritual and visual experience not to be missed, Madakalipura invites you to experience Indonesia's extraordinary natural beauty.

Malang, East Java

Pantai Batu Bengkung

Batu Bengkung Beach in Malang is a hidden paradise with white sand and captivating waves. Surrounded by steep cliffs, the beach offers spectacular views and a serene atmosphere. Perfect for relaxing, taking pictures or exploring its natural beauty. Don't miss the mesmerising sunset here!
